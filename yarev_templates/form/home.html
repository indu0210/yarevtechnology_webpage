About Us

Yarev Technology Pvt. Ltd is start up incubated under Foundation for Technology & Business Incubation(FTBI), NIt Rourkela which is focusing on manufacturing disruptive solution in the field of home automation, water quality monitoring and water conservation etc.

The company has been formed with an objective of making people's life easier by providing them efficeint solution for the day to day problems like water tank filling and also and also aims at preventing them from the health issues due to bad quality of water by providing them the monitoring of the quality of water in the tank and solutions for it.

We are persently building automatic building switches with IOT applications and water quality monitoring to provide home automation based solutions for households and industries at best prices.

The first and the leadership product of the company-YOPO is one such product.Small in size and affordable by everyone,it will solve the number of problems faced by the people and the enironment like wastage of water and electricity to wastage of time and physical effort.

The company aims to provide the products that seeks to solve the everyday problems of the majority of the population at best prices and make their life easy.
